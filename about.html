About

Craig Mills, SMP Practitioner

My name is Craig Mills and I’m the sole practitioner here at SMP of Lexington. I’m a lifelong resident of Lexington who began losing hair in my early twenties. By 32, when I first discovered SMP, I was somewhere between a 4 and 5 rating on the Norwood Balding Scale. After experiencing the same questions and stress you may be experiencing for yourself, I finally committed to undergoing SMP treatment in 2013 - and, wow, was I satisfied. The results were so impressive, I committed to provide this life-bolstering service to other people anxious and dissatisfied about their hair loss. I decided to receive training from one of the top SMP pioneers and practitioners in the world, Matt Iulo of Scalp Micro USA in Los Angeles. After completing my training with Matt, I immediately flew back home to start SMP of Lexington. If you’re considering SMP, don’t hesitate to reach out to me via phone, text or email. We can meet for a free consultation and discuss the SMP treatment needed for your level of hair loss. If you’re unable to make it into the office, I can usually provide a quote via text or email based on photos of your scalp. Together we can figure out a treatment plan that’s right for you.
